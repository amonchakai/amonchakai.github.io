Hg10 Policy

General information

Hg10 is an open-source XMPP client for BlackBerry 10. It enables you to connect to any server which use the XMPP protocol. This includes Google Hangout, Facebook messenger, Jabber, ...
The main focus of this application is Google Hangout, however some support for Facebook messenger is also supported

Access to the source code:

The application is an open-source project under the GNU GPL licence. You can access to the code, edit it, modify it.

  • Souce code

Privacy:

Using this application, you can connect to your Google or Facebook account. These are sensitive data. This application will never share any private information. All your data always stay on your device. Your data will never be analysed, nor processed by this application. Everything stay on your phone. As previouly stated, the code of the application is open-source so you can check yourself what it does. No connection will never be made between your different accounts (Dropbox, Facebook, Google). The author of the application have no interest in your private data. Any access to your accounts is only meant to allow you to better interact with the service on which you are connected. Remeber, most of the asked permissions are optional. You can always reject them or refuse to connect.

Permission:

This application deal with differnt kind of permissions:

Permission asked on your BlackBerry device

The application will request several permissions

  • Access to your files is only for uploading your images, documents, ... to dropbox and share the url to your contact. Rejecting it will only affect the file attachment feature.
  • Headless: Headless permission is required for this application: the core application is in the headless process. Rejecting it would result in a non-working application

Permission asked for Google Hangout

The access to your gmail account can be provided into the settings. This is not mandatory and does not affect the instant messaging service. Access to email is ONLY meant to retrive instant message's history which are stored on your Google account. Only READ access is provided, NO modification can be done on your email account from the application. Moreover, Google API do not provide full access to your emails. No data is analysed... No data is send to any third party. The data always STAY on your device.
For your information, the handling of history is done by requesting the following request to Google: "is:chat friend_name". You can try it yourself on the gmail website.

Permission asked for Facebook

Currently, the application was not granted by Facebook to access to your account. Therefore no support for conversation history can be provided at the moment. In case you would connect to Facebook messenger, there is an access to your facebook account which can be provided into the settings. This is not mandatory and does not affect the instant messaging service. Currently there is even no added value of connecting to it. The handling of history may come if one day Facebook allow this application to access to your inbox data... In any case, the access to facebook is ONLY meant to retrive instant message's history which is stored in your inbox. The application only READ it and no data is further analysed... No data is send to any third party. The data always STAY on your device.

Permission asked for dropbox

The access to your dropbox account can be provided into the settings. This is not mandatory and does not affect the instant messaging service. Access to dropbox is only meant to host your files, images... on your dropbox account. The application has only access to a restricted folder on your dropbox account (Application/Hg10). For privacy reason, the author (me), do not want to go beyond this folder. Files stored in this folder are only accessible by you, and in case you share a file, the persons who recive the link from you.
